Hi, I’m Ishant Jadhav. I’ve been imagining worlds and stories for as long as I can remember. I love crafting experiences from horror to adventure. Below are a few projects from that journey.

THE DEFECTOR

Role: Designer and Developer

Genre: Historical Horror Fiction / Psychological Horror

Tagline: "Escape something worse than war"

Defector is a first-person historical horror game in development. It follows a German soldier trying to escape the war, whose journey becomes a psychological nightmare as he’s forced to face his past. It blends storytelling, atmosphere, and tension to explore the horrors of guilt and survival. As players move through the forest, they uncover memories, confront haunting visions, and try to escape something more terrifying than the war itself.

THE TARTARUS PROJECT

Role: Designer and Developer

Genre: Post-apocalyptic 3D platformer

Tagline: "Climb what’s left of the world."

The Tartarus Project is a 3D first-person platformer set in a dark, futuristic world where ancient mythology meets cyberpunk design. In a vast tower of traps and trials, you’ll rely on fast reflexes and parkour skills to survive and try to escape the endless cycle of punishment.

MORE ABOUT ME

I’m a game designer who likes to create full, meaningful experiences, not just flashy or complicated ones. For me, design is about balance and purpose. Every part of a game should exist for a reason.

I enjoy making story-driven, horror, open-world, and adventure games. I like building worlds that pull players in and make them feel something real. My goal is to make sure the story and gameplay always support each other so they feel like one thing, not two separate parts.

I work with Unreal Engine, Unity, Blender, Maya, Photoshop, and other tools to bring ideas to life. I’m good at imagining, improvising, solving problems, and managing teams to get the job done.

My belief is simple: Every detail and mechanic should help tell the story, and every story should fit naturally with the game. And with this belief, I want to become a name recognized in the industry, someone who creates games that leave a mark and stay with players for years.
